About Color BOPP Tape

Color BOPP Self Adhesive Tapes are widely used for packaging, branding and identification purposes. Available in multiple vibrant colors, these tapes help in easy segregation and professional presentation.

They offer excellent adhesion, durability and visual appeal for industrial and commercial packaging.

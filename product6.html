EPE FOAM ROLLS

About EPE Foam Rolls

EPE (Expanded Polyethylene) Foam Rolls are lightweight, flexible and highly protective packaging materials used for cushioning and surface protection.

They provide excellent shock absorption, moisture resistance and are widely used for fragile and premium product packaging.

Advantages

Superior Cushioning

Protects fragile items from impact.

Moisture Resistant

Prevents water damage.

Lightweight

Easy handling and transport.

Reusable

Eco-conscious packaging solution.

Uses

Electronics

Protection for TVs, appliances & gadgets.

Furniture

Scratch & dent protection.

Glassware

Safe packing of fragile items.

Industrial Packing

Heavy-duty cushioning use.

STRETCH FILMS

About Stretch Film

Stretch films are highly stretchable plastic films used to wrap and secure palletized goods during transportation and storage.

They provide excellent load stability, moisture protection and cost-effective packaging for industrial and warehouse operations.

Advantages

High Load Stability

Prevents shifting during transit.

Moisture Protection

Protects goods from dust and water.

Excellent Stretchability

Strong elastic memory.

Warehouse Friendly

Ideal for pallet wrapping.

Uses

Pallet Wrapping

Secures bulk shipments.

Transportation

Safe logistics movement.

Industrial Storage

Long-term protection.

Warehousing

Efficient stock handling.

MASKING TAPES

About Masking Tape

Masking tapes are pressure-sensitive tapes made for temporary surface protection during painting, polishing and finishing processes.

They ensure clean paint edges, easy removal and residue-free performance across multiple applications.

Advantages

Clean Removal

No residue after use.

Sharp Paint Lines

Perfect surface finish.

Surface Protection

Prevents paint damage.

Easy Application

Quick stick & remove.

Uses

Painting

Interior & exterior masking.

Automobile

Surface safety during painting.

Industrial Work

Temporary surface protection.

Home Improvement

DIY projects & repairs.

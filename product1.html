About Transparent BOPP Tape

Transparent BOPP Self Adhesive Tapes are versatile packaging solutions crafted from Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene film. With strong adhesive properties and exceptional clarity, they are ideal for securing parcels, protecting surfaces, and maintaining a clean, professional appearance.

These tapes offer excellent mechanical strength, resistance to UV light and moisture, and ensure consistent performance across a wide range of surfaces and temperatures.

Advantages

High Strength

Resists tearing and ensures safe packaging of goods.

Eco Friendly

Made from non-toxic, recyclable BOPP material.

Clear Visibility

Transparent surface keeps your branding visible.

Cost Effective

Optimal for bulk packaging operations.

Uses

Box Sealing

Secure parcels for shipping and storage.

Export Packaging

Ideal for export-quality packaging.

Document Lamination

Protect important documents professionally.

E-commerce

Safe and reliable for online order parcels.
